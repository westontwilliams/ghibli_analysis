SPIRITED AWAY 
Written & Directed by Hayao Miyazaki 
Adapted by Thomas Streed 
This is a fan-made screenplay adaptation of the 2001 Studio Ghibli  film Spirited Away (Sen to Chihiro no Kamikakushi). The reference  for dialogue is Disney's English dubbed version.
